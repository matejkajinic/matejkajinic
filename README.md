# Matej Kajinic

I am a research and thesis-driven engineer, investor, and founder. My work is focused on identifying the fundamental technological and infrastructural shifts that will define the next decade, sitting at the intersection of AI and energy.

My core thesis is that the future will be defined by two crucial currencies: **FLOPS (intelligence) & Watts (energy)**.

I hold an M.S. in Computer Science, where my research focused on applying neural networks to financial market trend prediction. I also hold a B.S. in Electrical Engineering where my research focused on exploring electromagnetic waves and their propagation, focusing on empirical propagation models.
